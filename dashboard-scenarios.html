MedAssist — All Patient Scenarios

For each scenario: what the frontdesk does · what the doctor sees on their dashboard · what changes in the database
Covers 4 requested scenarios + 5 additional edge cases that must be handled.

1
Patient booked an appointment — arrived on time ✅
Most common scenario in a well-run clinic. Everything works as expected.
IDEAL CASE
👩‍💼 Frontdesk Actions
1
Patient walks in and gives name. Frontdesk searches for their appointment in the upcoming list.
→ Frontdesk dashboard: "مواعيد اليوم" tab → sees محمود at 16:00
2
Clicks "وصل" (Arrived) on the appointment card. System records arrived_at = now() and adds patient to the doctor's queue.
→ Appointment status: scheduled → arrived
3
No further action needed. Doctor sees patient in their queue immediately.
⚡ Frontdesk effort: minimal. One button tap. The system handles the rest.

2
Walk-in patient — no appointment 🚶
Patient shows up without booking. Very common in Egyptian clinics. Frontdesk decides whether to add them.
WALK-IN
👩‍💼 Frontdesk Actions
1
Patient walks in with no booking. Frontdesk checks if doctor has capacity today (free slots visible in schedule).
→ Frontdesk checks doctor's schedule for gaps between appointments
2
Clicks "إضافة زيارة عادية" (Add walk-in). Searches for patient by name/phone. If new patient, registers them first.
→ If new patient: quick registration form (name, phone, age, sex)
3
Selects visit type (كشف / متابعة / طارئ) and confirms. Patient enters queue immediately. Frontdesk tells patient to wait.
→ Queue counter increases by 1 on doctor's dashboard instantly
⚠️ Frontdesk has authority to accept or turn away walk-ins based on capacity. System should show how many scheduled patients are still coming.

3
Appointment patient — arrived late ⏱️
Patient had a 16:00 appointment, showed up at 16:45. Doctor may have already moved on. System must handle queue position correctly.
LATE ARRIVAL
👩‍💼 Frontdesk Actions
1
Patient arrives at 16:45 for a 16:00 appointment. System highlights the appointment as "متأخر" (late) automatically — any appointment past its start time with no arrival is flagged amber.
→ Appointment card shows: "متأخر بـ 45 دقيقة" in amber
2
Frontdesk clicks "وصل متأخراً". A decision dialog appears:
a) Add to end of queue (fair to others)
b) Squeeze in if doctor is between patients
c) Reschedule to another day
→ Dialog: "وصل متأخراً بـ 45 دقيقة — كيف تريد التعامل معه؟"
3
If added to queue: placed at END regardless of original slot, with amber "متأخر" badge. Wait time counts from arrival, not from appointment time.
→ queue.priority = 0 (lower than normal), queue.late = true
Key rule: late patients lose their time priority. They go to end of queue. Doctor can override if they choose to see them sooner.

5
No-show — patient never arrives 👻
Appointment time passed. Patient didn't come. Slot is wasted. System should auto-flag and give frontdesk options.
NO-SHOW
👩‍💼 Frontdesk Actions
1
System auto-flags after grace period (default: 20 min past appointment time). Appointment turns red on frontdesk screen with "غياب محتمل" alert.
2
Frontdesk can call the patient via phone number shown on card. Then chooses:
a) "تأكيد الغياب" → marks as no_show
b) "في الطريق" → extends grace period 15 more min
3
If confirmed no-show: slot becomes available. Frontdesk can offer it to a waiting walk-in or next appointment patient.
System can optionally auto-send an SMS: "لم تحضر لموعدك. هل تريد إعادة الجدولة؟"

6
Patient arrives very early (45+ min before slot) 🐦
Patient has a 17:00 appointment but arrives at 15:30. Doctor is still busy with other patients. Should not disrupt the schedule.
EARLY
👩‍💼 Frontdesk Actions
1
Patient arrives 90 minutes early. Frontdesk finds their appointment in "مواعيد اليوم" — it shows "بعد 90 دقيقة".

b) Hold — enter at their scheduled time
→ Option B recommended: keeps schedule predictable for doctor
3
If "Hold" selected: patient is marked as "حاضر — ينتظر دوره" but not added to queue yet. Queue entry auto-fires at their appointment time if doctor is free.
Frontdesk should communicate wait time clearly to patient: "موعدك في الساعة ٥، نضيفك للقائمة عند الساعة ٤:٥٠"

7
Emergency walk-in — jumps the queue 🚨
Patient arrives in urgent distress. Must be seen immediately regardless of queue order. Frontdesk escalates.
EMERGENCY
👩‍💼 Frontdesk Actions
1
Patient arrives in clear distress. Frontdesk clicks "إضافة طارئ" — prominent red button always visible on frontdesk screen.
2
Minimal data entry: just name + phone (or skip if truly urgent). Confirm as "طارئ". Patient enters queue with priority = 9 (highest).
→ Doctor dashboard: emergency card appears at TOP with 🚨 red banner
3
Doctor gets a push notification (if on another screen) — "مريض طارئ في الانتظار". Existing patient session can be paused.
⚠️ Emergency button bypasses all validation. Frontdesk has full authority. The system records who added the emergency for audit trail.

8
Doctor is running behind — cascade delay ⏰
Doctor took longer with a patient. Next appointment patient arrives on time but will wait extra. System should show delay to frontdesk so they can communicate.
DELAY
👩‍💼 Frontdesk Actions
1
System detects delay automatically when a session runs past its scheduled duration. Shows frontdesk: "الدكتور متأخر بـ 20 دقيقة — المرضى القادمون سيتأثرون"
2
Frontdesk communicates delay to waiting patients verbally. Can optionally send SMS to upcoming appointment patients: "الدكتور متأخر قليلاً، موعدك سيبدأ الساعة ١٧:٢٠"
3
Cascading delay shown on appointment timeline: all future appointments shift by the estimated delay minutes.
Frontdesk is the patient-facing communicator. The system gives them the delay data — they use it to manage expectations and avoid complaints.

Queue Priority Rules — How Patients Are Ordered in "في الانتظار الآن"
Patient Type Priority Score Queue Position Rule Badge Shown Who Sets Priority
🚨 Emergency walk-in 9 Always first. Separate red banner above queue. طارئ Frontdesk (manual)
✅ Appointment — on time 2 Sorted by appointment time, then by arrival time within same slot. كشف Auto (system)
🚶 Walk-in 1 After all appointment patients. Sorted by arrival time. بدون موعد Auto (system)
⏱️ Late arrival 0 Last in queue. Treated as walk-in effectively. متأخر Auto (system)
🐦 Early arrival (promoted) 2 Gets original appointment priority when their slot arrives. مبكر Auto at appointment time
Queue Sort Formula (SQL ORDER BY)
ORDER BY
  priority DESC,                  -- 9=emergency first
  COALESCE(appointment.start_time, '9999') ASC,  -- appointments by slot time
  checked_in_at ASC                -- within same priority, FIFO
